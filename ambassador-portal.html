What materials do you use?

We use medical-grade dental gold (10K, 14K, 18K), sterling silver, and can work with white gold and platinum. All materials are hypoallergenic and safe for your mouth.

How long does it take?

Standard orders take 2-3 weeks. Rush delivery ($150 extra) saves 4+ days. We'll keep you updated throughout the process!

Where do I get my mold done?

Jack of All Fades (Mondays) or Global Goods Shop (Saturdays). Text (530) 214-0676 to schedule your appointment.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, Zelle, Venmo, CashApp, and PayPal. Online payments have small transaction fees.

Do grillz tarnish?

Our dental gold has 0% tarnish guarantee! Silver may tarnish over time but can be easily cleaned. We provide care instructions with every order.

How much do grillz cost?

Prices vary by material and design. Silver starts at $55/tooth, 10K gold at $200/tooth, 14K dental gold at $310/tooth. Check our pricing page for full details!
